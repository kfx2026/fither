About FitHer

Science-based weight loss for women - free, always

Our Mission

FitHer was created to fill a critical gap: high-quality, science-based weight loss resources that are completely free. We believe every woman deserves access to accurate, evidence-based information about her health.

What Makes Us Different

  • 100% Free: All tools, guides, and plans
  • Science-Based: Peer-reviewed research
  • Products: Full support guide
  • Women-Centered: PCOS, menopause, hormones
