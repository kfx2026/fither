Privacy Policy

Your privacy is built in — not an afterthought

Last updated: May 27, 2026

📌 The Short Version

FitHer collects ZERO personal data. Period. We do not track you, profile you, sell your data, or even ask who you are. All our calculators run entirely in your browser — nothing you type ever leaves your device.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

None. We intentionally do not collect any personal information whatsoever. This includes:

  • ❌ No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • ❌ No account creation or login
  • ❌ No tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or pixel tags
  • ❌ No IP address logging or fingerprinting
  • ❌ No third-party trackers (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
  • ❌ No comments, forums, or user-generated content storage

2. How Do Our Calculators Work?

All calculation logic — BMI, body fat, calorie needs, macros, calorie burn — runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server. You can verify this by checking your browser's network tab: zero outbound requests when you use any tool on this site.

3. Cookies

This site uses no tracking cookies. A minimal cookie banner is displayed as a privacy best practice, but no cookies are actually placed for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes. The site functions fully without any cookie consent.

4. External Content

Some pages display images loaded from Unsplash, a free stock photography service. When your browser loads these images, Unsplash may receive your IP address as part of the standard HTTP request. Unsplash has its own privacy policy regarding this data. We do not control or have access to any data Unsplash may collect.

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect your data, there is nothing to secure, leak, or breach. Your privacy is absolute. This site is served over HTTPS, ensuring that the content you view is not tampered with in transit.

6. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and Beyond)

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or port. Privacy regulations exist to protect you — and we've designed FitHer so those protections are automatic and absolute.

  • GDPR (EU): Article 17 "Right to erasure" is trivially satisfied — we have nothing to erase.
  • CCPA (California): No personal information is collected, sold, or shared.
  • Any jurisdiction: Your privacy is 100% intact when using this site.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect that. However, our commitment to zero data collection will not change.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out through our Contact page.
